The Best Movies According to John

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

The Shawshank Redemption

A masterpiece of storytelling with exceptional performances.

Unforgiven

A brilliantly written and directed film, featuring a stellar cast and superb acting.

Interstellar

A breathtaking sci-fi epic with solid scientific foundations, stunning cinemotography, and a great soundtrack!

Note:

As a big movie lover, I often categorize my faviorites by directors. Some of my top picks, in no particular order, include Clint Eastwood, David Fincher, Christopher Nolan, and Michael Mann. I'm also a huge fan of nearly everything produced or distributed by A24.
